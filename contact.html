Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cleaning services.

Booking & Services

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you do not need to be home. Many of our clients provide us with a key, lockbox code, or smart lock access. We are fully insured and our staff is vetted for your peace of mind.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes! We bring professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly cleaning supplies. If you have specific products you prefer us to use, just let us know and we will happily use them.

Are you pet friendly?

Absolutely. We love pets! We use safe, non-toxic products. Please let us know if you have pets so we can ensure they are safe and comfortable during our visit.

Pricing & Payment

How do I pay for the service?

We accept all major credit cards, e-transfers, and cheques for commercial clients. Payment is typically processed after the cleaning is completed to your satisfaction.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We understand that schedules change. We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment may incur a $50 fee to cover our cleaners' time.

What if I am not satisfied with the cleaning?

Your satisfaction is our priority (100% Guaranteed). If you are not happy with any aspect of our service, please contact us within 24 hours and we will return to re-clean the specific area at no extra cost.

Safety & Insurance

Are your cleaners insured and bonded?

Yes, CMG Cleaning Solutions carries full liability insurance and all our employees are bonded. We also ensure all staff are covered by WSIB.

Do you perform background checks?

Yes, every member of our team undergoes a rigorous vetting process, including criminal background checks and reference verifications, before entering any client's home or office.
