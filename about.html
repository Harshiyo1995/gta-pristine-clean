About Our Company

Dedicated to excellence since 2015.

Our Mission

Creating Healthier Environments

At GTA Pristine Clean, our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, reliable cleaning services that improve the quality of life for our clients. We believe a clean home is a happy home, and a clean office is a productive one.

We started in 2015 as a small family business in Mississauga and have grown to serve the entire Greater Toronto Area, thanks to our commitment to detailed work and customer satisfaction.
